Privacy Policy

The legal bits.


A statement from cbtec.org protecting your privacy and helping you understand how we use your information. By law, we are required to provide this information if we collect any information about the user, even if it involves storing something as simple as cookies or E-Mail addresses for mailing lists. We will link privacy policies from other services we use on the site to provide your experience.


How we Collect & Use your information

Mailing Lists and Newsletters collect your E-Mail address via the Mail Chimp service. Their privacy policy listed here: https://mailchimp.com/legal/privacy/ . Your data is stored through their service and then used to send automated newsletters when signed up to. When you input your details to any field on the site you are agreeing to the privacy policy of our services and the ones of the services we use to give you the experience you have today. We use G-Mail to send our Newsletters on the E-Mail: businesscbtec@gmail.com and only on that E-Mail address. G-Mail privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en


Cookies


Our site also uses cookies to function. Cookies outputted directly from cbtec.org are mandatory for the site to function, they cannot be disabled. Cookies outputted from the code of cbtec.org : Cookie policy accept prompt – This cookie remembers if you accepted the cookie agreement on the home page of the site. GitHub provides cookies to the site which are mandatory for function and can also not be disabled. YouTube provides cookies to function, collect data and other; YouTubes Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en-GB . Twitch provides cookies to function, collect data and other; Twitch’s Privacy Policy: https://www.twitch.tv/p/en/legal/privacy-notice/ .


Advertising Networks & Other

Currently on cbtec.org we do not use any Advertising agencies/networks or services.

Opting In-Out of Data Collection

While on cbtec.org , you may request opting out of data collection. To Opt out of Mailing Lists/Newsletters, respond to the Newsletter with “OPT-OUT” and your E-Mail address will be removed from our systems until you Opt in to these services again. When Opt-Out of a service, we will not store your data longer than 5-7 days.

Requesting your Data

You may request your data via E-Mail at businesscbtec@gmail.com for free and a document with all the data we collected about you will be sent as soon as an agent is available to respond.

Deleting your Data

You may also request data deletion via E-Mail at businesscbtec@gmail.com for free and you will be sent a document with all the data we collected about you as soon as an agent is available to respond. After the agent has responded to your E-Mail, your data will be removed from our systems within a 24 hour time frame. This includes your support threads.
